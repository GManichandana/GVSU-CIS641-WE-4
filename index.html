Diabetes Risk Prediction System

Diabetes is a common disease affecting a significant part of the population. It is a global concern, but many people are unaware of how to manage it. Diabetes occurs when the level of glucose in the blood, often called hyperglycemia, rises above the required level. The inability of the pancreas to produce the required insulin or the inability to use it properly to convert glucose into energy is the cause of diabetes. Diabetes can be classified into three major types: Diabetes Mellitus, Diabetes Insipidus, and Gestational Diabetes. This project is a data science project in which we predict whether a given data point is diabetic or not.
